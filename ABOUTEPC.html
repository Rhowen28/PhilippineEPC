Philippine EPC Integrity Technology, Inc

Our Story

Integrity Technology Co. is founded in 1993. In the early period, company undertook government civil engineering and construction related project.

  • Integrity Technology Co. core business is mainly in wireless telecom. RF Planning, Network Optimization, Project Management, BTS/Core/HUB site Installation, maintenance as well as Core/OMC Site troubleshoot, Engineering audit and management, Technical engineer dispatch, passive component OEM and sales.
  • Since the establishment of the company, we have always adhered to the philosophy of ”expert team, quality service”, and continue to provide customers with high-quality services and actively training program for young engineer.
  • In 2012, we established Hong Kong Integrity Technology Co., Ltd. Through Hong Kong Integrity Technology Co. we invest in Philippines Integrity Technology Inc. 100% shares in 2019. We also invest in 1 listed company and 2 local companies in Taiwan to diversify our operations.

Philippine EPC Integrity Technology Inc. was founded with a goal to own and operate high quality wireless infrastructure sites around the world.
